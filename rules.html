Ryder Cup Rules

  • You can fluff your ball
  • You can move your ball from man made objects. Not trees, bushes, etc.
  • One breakfast ball on the first tee, but you have to use it
  • Perfect Provisionals: After you hit a drive OB, drop in the fairway where it went out shooting 3
  • Remove ball from bunkers if there are puddles
  • No mulligans
  • Gimmie putts handled by each foursome
  • All rules listed above are not set in stone and will be voted on by everyone
